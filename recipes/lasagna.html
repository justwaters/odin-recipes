Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has captured the hearts and appetites of food lovers around the world. Characterized by its layered structure, lasagna typically consists of sheets of pasta interspersed with rich fillings, such as a hearty meat sauce, creamy béchamel, or a robust marinara, along with a generous sprinkling of cheese. The most common cheese used is ricotta, often blended with egg and herbs, while mozzarella and Parmesan are essential for that gooey, golden-brown top. The dish is often baked until bubbling and fragrant, creating a delightful contrast between the tender pasta, savory fillings, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Herbs
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions
  3. Mix ricotta cheese with egg and herbs
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of a baking dish
  5. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce
  6. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese mixture on top of the noodles
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used up
  8. Top with mozzarella cheese and bake until golden brown
